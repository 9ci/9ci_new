9ci - Solutions for your Accounts Receivable Problems

Our software offers full invoice-to-payment management of accounts receivable. With a easy dashboard, flexible systems, intelligent automation, and powerful reports, we give you everything you need to get paid faster.

And we don’t just sell you software. No canned products here. Instead, our systems can be custom-tailored to the way your company does business. And we offer extensive consulting on how to make it all work perfectly for you.

We provide solutions

  • Intelligent Auto Cash Application

    Cash Application So Smart, It's Almost Scary. Apply cash in minutes, not days, with intelligent import of bank data & images. Intelligent payment matching. Automated cash application. Automated &amb; intelligent payment correction.

  • Dispute & Deductions Management

    Disputes Automated to Make Your Work Easy. Resolve disputes ASAP. Get invoices paid on-time. Manage & reduce disputes. Recover invalid deductions & chargebacks.

  • Collections Management

    Reduce Everything – DSO, Bad Debt, Wasted Time & Manpower Get paid faster. Reduce DSO, 30+ Day Delinquencies, Unresolved Credits & Deductions. And automated workflow means you need less time and staff.

  • Greenbill Online Invoice & Payment Portal

    Easy for Customers to Use. Easy for Customers to Love. Customer self-service invoice & payment portal means faster delivery, fewer mailing costs, and better customer service with an easy-to-navigate portal.
